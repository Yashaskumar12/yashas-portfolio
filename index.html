01 · About

I'm a Computer Science undergraduate at Dayananda Sagar University, Bangalore, focused on the intersection of applied AI and production engineering. My work centers on Retrieval-Augmented Generation pipelines, autonomous agents, and adaptive systems — built with FastAPI, LangChain, and vector stores like FAISS and Neo4j.

Beyond the model layer, I care about the parts that make a system actually usable: secure auth flows (JWT, OAuth 2.0), containerized deployments on AWS EC2, and CI/CD pipelines through Jenkins and GitHub Actions.
